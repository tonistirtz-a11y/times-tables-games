✅ The Grid

Tap the numbers in counting order — the glowing line shows what's next.

👋Pick a times table and press Start — can you lock the vault in time?
How to play
  1. The robber is cracking the safe! Press Start and the countdown begins.
  2. Fill the grid in counting order (1×, 2×, 3×…): the glowing line shows which fact is next — tap its answer in the number row below. Numbers go down each column, just like the 9 lines.
  3. Each correct tap is +10 points. A wrong tap costs 2 seconds — so think, don't guess!
  4. Fill all nine before time runs out to slam the vault shut: +50 bonus plus 2 points for every second left. Beat your own best time! Choose 🧘 Practice for no timer at all.
