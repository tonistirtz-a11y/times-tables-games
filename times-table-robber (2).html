How to play
  1. Choose a times table (2–9). The nine answers are hiding in the mystery cards — plus one sneaky robber!
  2. Grab a card to flip it over, then drag the number onto the line where it belongs, counting in order down each column.
  3. Drop it on the right line and the multiplication fact appears — that's +10 points! Drop it on a wrong line and the card bounces back so you can try again.
  4. Place all nine before you flip the robber to win the round for a +50 bonus. If the robber pops up, he steals the numbers back and the cards reshuffle!
