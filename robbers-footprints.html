How to play
  1. Study the grid — the numbers count in order down each column. Then the robber dashes through and steals some, leaving 👣 footprints!
  2. Look at the lineup and figure out which numbers are really missing. Use the neighbors to help: what comes between the numbers you can still see?
  3. Drag a suspect onto its footprint. The right number on the right line reveals the fact and earns +10 points. Watch out — some suspects are fakes that don't belong on the grid at all!
  4. Recover every stolen number to solve the case for a +25 bonus. Level up: Rookie loses 2 numbers, Detective 3, and Master Sleuth 4 with extra-tricky fakes!
