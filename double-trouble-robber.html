How to play
  1. Choose a times table for the Blue House and one for the Purple House. All eighteen answers are shuffled into the mystery cards — plus one sneaky robber!
  2. Grab a card to flip it, then decide: which house does this number belong to? Drag it onto its line, counting in order down each column. Some numbers fit in both houses!
  3. A correct drop shows the multiplication fact and earns +10 points. A wrong drop bounces back so you can try again.
  4. Flip the robber and he robs one house — those numbers shuffle back into the pile! Fill both houses to win the round for a +100 bonus.
