How to play
  1. Study the grid while it's face-up — the answers count in order down each column, just like the 9 lines. Then the cards flip over and the robber hides behind one number!
  2. Read the WANTED fact, like 4 × 5, and tap the hideout where its answer lives. Right spot = the card flips up, shows the fact, and you earn +10 points. Wrong spot just shakes — try again!
  3. Sooner or later you'll flip the robber's hideout — GOTCHA! Catching him ends the round with a +50 reward.
  4. Stuck? Tap Peek to see the grid for 2 seconds (it costs 5 points). Level up: Rookie asks the facts in order, Detective mixes them up, and Master Sleuth gives no study time at all!
